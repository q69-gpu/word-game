📖 How to Play ▼
  1. Enter your name, pick a difficulty, time limit & min word length.
  2. Type a valid English word and submit it.
  3. Qbit responds with a word starting with the last letter of yours.
  4. Your next word must start with Qbit's last letter.
  5. No repeating words. Submit before the timer runs out!
  6. Earn power-ups: ❄️ Freeze, 💎 Double, 🔄 Skip, 🔤 Letter Pick!
  7. Use 🔤 Letter Pick to choose Qbit's starting letter!
  8. Tap any used word to see its definition.
